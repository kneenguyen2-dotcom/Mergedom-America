# Mergedom: America

<!-- Core concept and historical scope -->
A U.S. history discovery game where players combine basic elements to create key events, ideas, and inventions—exploring the story of America from early exploration to the modern era. 

<!-- Learning objective -->
Players can visually understand how different historical ideas, resources, and systems combine to shape civilizations.

<!-- Technical/platform overview -->
This is a browser-based Little Alchemy-inspired game. Explore history through connections.

<!-- Core gameplay loop -->
Mix, match, and build the story of America!

<!-- Main mechanic -->
In this Little Alchemy–inspired game, combine ideas and discoveries to unlock key moments and concepts from U.S. history.

<!-- Player experience goal -->
Experiment your way through the nation’s past, one creation at a time.
